# 🚀 SELLO

> **Your AI Business Development Employee.**
>
> SELLO finds leads, researches prospects, starts conversations, qualifies customers, and tells you exactly when to take over.

---

## 🌟 Vision

Businesses spend countless hours searching for clients across Reddit, X, forums, and communities.

SELLO automates the entire early sales process using AI.

Instead of hiring a team to:

- Find leads
- Research companies
- Send outreach
- Qualify prospects
- Follow up
- Organize conversations

SELLO works 24/7 as your AI sales employee.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🔍 AI Lead Discovery

Continuously discovers people looking for services like:

- Website Development
- Mobile App Development
- UI/UX Design
- Content Creation
- Social Media Management
- AI Automation
- SEO
- Marketing
- Video Editing
- Any custom service

Supports:

- Reddit
- X (Twitter)
- More platforms (Coming Soon)

---

## 🧠 AI Intent Detection

SELLO understands intent instead of relying only on keywords.

Example:

"I need a website"

"My website is outdated."

"Can anyone recommend a developer?"

"Our agency is looking for freelancers."

All are detected as potential leads.

---

## 🌍 Company Research

Before contacting anyone, SELLO researches publicly available information such as:

- Website
- Business
- Industry
- Tech Stack
- Social Presence
- Recent Activity

---

## 📊 AI Lead Score

Every lead gets scored.

Example:

Lead Score: 97/100

Buying Intent: High

Urgency: High

Budget Confidence: Medium

Recommendation:
Contact Immediately

---

## 💬 AI Conversation

SELLO communicates naturally.

Instead of templates,
it understands context and responds like a human.

It can:

- Answer questions
- Ask qualifying questions
- Remember conversations
- Handle objections
- Follow up

---

## 👤 Human Takeover

AI knows when humans should step in.

Examples:

- Client asks pricing
- Wants a meeting
- Requests contract
- Wants payment
- High-value opportunity

One click transfers the conversation to a human.

Another click returns control back to AI.

---

## 📥 Unified Inbox

Manage every conversation from one dashboard.

Instead of opening multiple platforms,
everything is centralized.

---

## 🧠 AI Memory

Every client gets a memory profile.

Stores:

- Previous conversations
- Business details
- Pain points
- Budget
- Interests
- Current stage

The AI never forgets context.

---

## 📈 Analytics

Track everything.

- Leads Found
- Qualified Leads
- Conversations
- Conversion Rate
- Revenue
- AI Performance
- Human Takeovers
- Platform Performance

Examples:
